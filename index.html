Some random information

The Arie Family (아리에 가문, Alie Gamun) is one the Ten Great Families and its head is Arie Hon. It is currently the strongest and most famous family.[

The Khun Family (쿤 가문, Kun Gamun) is one of the 10 Great Families. The head of the Khun Family, Khun Eduan, is said to have the most "wives" (married partners) of the 10 Great Families, so the family itself is very large.

The Po Bidau Family (포 비더 가문, Po Bideo Gamun) is one of the 10 Great Families led by Po Bidau Gustang.

The Yeon Family (연 가문, Yeon Gamun) is one of the 10 Great Families lead by Yeon Yirang. Interestingly enough, members of the family do not carry her blood as all of them are born from Ilarde, the "godmother" of the clan.[2]
